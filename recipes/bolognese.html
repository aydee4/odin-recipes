Bolognese

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make a delicious bolognese.

Ingredients

  • Pasta/Bolognese
  • Salt
  • Bolognese Sauce
  • Seasonings

Steps

Pasta/Spaghetti

  1. Bring water in a pot to a boil.
  2. Add a generous amount of salt to the water.
  3. Add your desired amount of pasta.
  4. Boil your pasta until it is cooked.

Sauce

  1. Add your pasta sauce of choice to your pot/pan and season appropriately.
  2. Cook sauce.

ENJOY :)
