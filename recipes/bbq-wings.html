BBQ Wings

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make a succulent bbq wings.

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Mixed herbs
  • White pepper
  • Aromat seasoning
  • Chicken seasoning
  • Smoked paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Hot sauce
  • Honey
  • Barbecue sauce

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine mixed herbs, white pepper, aromat seasoning, chicken seasoning, smoked paprika, garlic powder, oil, and flour.
  2. Coat the wings.
  3. Place the wings in your air fryer in a single layer.
  4. Cook for 40 minutes, flipping halfway through, until skin is crispy and golden brown.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the BBQ sauce, the hot sauce, and the honey. Stir the cooked wings in the sauce and coat them evenly.
